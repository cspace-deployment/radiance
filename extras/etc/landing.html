Blacklight "demos" for 5 UCB Museums

Caveat lector!

These are demos, and not everything works and many bits are speculative or sketchy! In particular:

  • Blacklight is a popular open-source "discovery framework"; click here to see a list of sites that use Blacklight. (A tile for PAHMA's portal appears at the bottom.)
  • The are based on (i.e. use the data from) the existing public Solr cores which provision the existing Django portals.
  • The fields that appear -- in facets, displays, etc. -- are those that appear in the "legacy" Django portals, but not in the same order; indeed, the order of fields is at the moment pretty random.
  • 15 images have been selected at random to use in the 4 x 4 grid on the "splash pages".
  • The links in those 15 images do not take you to the object corresponding to the image, but to a more generic search for something of which that image is an example.
  • The boilerplate text in the first cell is borrowed from PAHMA's Blacklight Portal.
  • This feature is merely a stand-in for what you'd LIKE to see on this page. Please use your imagination!
  • (This can all be changed: contact jblowe@berkeley.edu if you'd like details, or to make changes.)
